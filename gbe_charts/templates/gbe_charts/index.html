Legacy Growing Beyond Earth data

Fairchild Gardens runs the Growing Beyond Earth Project (GBE), where schools grow plants to determine the best candidates for the International Space Station. This site is an extraction of the school's spreadsheet data into MongoDB and Ploty, and then applying various queries and charting techniques. Data here is from the 2020 school year.
The code and results here are my personal experimentation and has no formal relationship to Fairchild Gardens or NASA. The purpose is to research coding and display techniques, and does not focus on scientific results.
